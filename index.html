Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

QuickFit ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

QuickFit stores all your workout data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. Your workout history, statistics, and preferences remain entirely on your device.

Local Data Stored

  • Workout history (exercises, dates, durations)
  • App preferences (sound, voice guidance, notifications)
  • Statistics (total workouts, streaks)
  • Premium subscription status

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements to free users. Google may collect certain data for ad personalization, including your device's advertising identifier and approximate location (IP-based), as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings. Premium users do not see advertisements.

Third-Party Services

QuickFit uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — advertising for free users
  • Google Play Services — app distribution

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data. The only third-party data sharing occurs through the Google AdMob SDK, which may collect device identifiers for advertising purposes.

Data Security

All user data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. No data is transmitted to external servers. Internet access is used solely for loading advertisements.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by going to Settings > Clear All Data in the app. This permanently removes all workout history, statistics, and preferences. Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data. Since no data is stored on external servers, deletion is immediate and complete.

Children's Privacy

QuickFit does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences interested in fitness and is not directed at children.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (visible within the app's History and Stats screens)
  • Delete your data (Settings > Clear All Data)
  • Export your data (Settings > Export Workout Data)
  • Opt out of personalized ads (via your device's ad settings)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at quickfit.app.support@gmail.com
